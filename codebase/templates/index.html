What is Hobby Connect?

Hobby Connect is a social platform that allows hobbyist and clubs to host their meetings. We provide a safe and user friendly way to meet virtually. If you are looking for friends with common interest try Hobby Connect. You can join for free and begin exploring clubs from all over the world.

How do we ensure your safety?

When you join the Hobby Connect family your safety is our top priority. We will never share any of the information you give us. No one will have access to your email account, or your first and last name. We will only show memeber your username. We also give parents oversite of all of their childrens activity within the Hobby Connect website ith our new Overwatch feature. When making an account for your child just simply check the "Child Account" box and we will walk you through creating a parent account.
